# Apollo Lead Enrichment Automation using Zapier

Automatically collect, enrich, organize, and notify your team of new qualified leads using Apollo, Zapier, and Slack.

---

# 📖 Overview

This project automates the lead enrichment process by collecting prospect information from Apollo, organizing qualified leads, and instantly notifying the sales team through Slack.

The workflow eliminates repetitive manual tasks and ensures new opportunities are shared with the team in real time.

---

# 💼 Business Problem

Sales teams often spend valuable time researching leads, organizing contact information, and manually notifying team members about new prospects.

This manual process slows down outreach and increases the risk of missed opportunities.

---

# 💡 Solution

Built a Zapier automation that:

- Retrieves qualified prospect data from Apollo
- Processes and enriches lead information
- Organizes lead records
- Sends real-time Slack notifications
- Reduces manual data entry and improves sales response time

---

# 🔄 Workflow

```text
New Leads
      │
      ▼
Apollo
      │
      ▼
Retrieve Qualified Leads
      │
      ▼
Zapier
      │
      ├──────────────► Slack Notification
      │
      ▼
Process & Organize Leads
      │
      ▼
Google Sheets
```
